Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar (born 22 June 1974), known mononymously as Vijay or by his moniker Thalapathy (Commander), is one of India's most commercially successful actors and is currently a prominent political figure in Tamil Nadu. Current Political Career In February 2024, Vijay officially entered politics by launching his party, Tamilaga Vettri Kazhagam (TVK). Retirement from Cinema: He announced that he would retire from acting after his 69th film to focus entirely on politics for the 2026 Tamil Nadu Assembly elections. Ideology: His party's ideology is described as "Secular Social Justice," incorporating values from Ambedkarism, Periyarism, and Marxism. Recent Events: In September 2025, a tragic crowd crush at a TVK rally in Karur resulted in over 40 deaths, for which Vijay later met with victim families and provided financial aid. Cinema & Music Stardom Spanning over three decades, Vijay's film career is defined by massive box-office dominance and high-energy performances. Major Hits: His career-defining blockbusters include Ghilli (2004), Pokkiri (2007), Thuppakki (2012), Mersal (2017), and Leo (2023), the latter being the highest-grossing film of his career at over ₹600 crore. Final Films: His penultimate film, The Greatest of All Time (2024), grossed over ₹450 crore. His farewell film, titled Jana Nayagan, is scheduled for release in January 2026. Music: An accomplished playback singer, he has recorded over 30 songs, including the global hits "Google Google" and "Selfie Pulla." Personal Life & Philanthropy Family: He is the son of film director S. A. Chandrasekhar and singer Shoba Chandrasekhar. He married Sangeetha Sornalingam in 1999; they have two children, Jason Sanjay and Divya Saasha. Welfare: Long before formal politics, he operated the Vijay Makkal Iyakkam, a social welfare organization that provided relief during natural disasters like Cyclone Thane and the Kerala floods. Recognition: He was awarded an honorary doctorate from Dr. M.G.R. Educational and Research Institute in 2007 for his achievements in cinema and philanthropy. Other Notable Vijays: Vijay Sethupathi: A prolific Tamil actor known for versatile roles; recently hosted Bigg Boss Tamil Season 9 (Finale: Jan 2026). Deverakonda Vijay: A popular Telugu actor known for Arjun Reddy and Geetha Govindam. A. L. Vijay: A Tamil film director known for Madrasapattinam and Thalaivii. Vijay Amritraj: A legendary Indian tennis player and sports commentator.
